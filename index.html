What is Koshish Family?

Koshish family is non-profit organization which was founder by Sandeep kumar and Amit Sir who were former student of DIT University. Today I will share my experience of NGO. This is one of the best things which is very close to my heart. In my NGO we used to teach underprivileged children by providing them quality education. We not only care about their education but also focus on their overall development. Whenever I spend some time with these lovely kids, I realize that how grateful I am. We have everything, a good home, sufficient food, a good college and school, a good society, and a good environment. But when I look over the life of those children it is very struggling. Their parents struggle a lot to even provide two meals a day to their children. They do not have enough resources like shelter, pure water, food, clothes, good and healthy environment. Although these children are very talented and have a very sharp mind,but just due to the lack of good education and good school, they do not get enough opportunity to showcase their knowledge and talent. But our NGO named Koshish Family has taken the full responsibility of these children. We try to complete all of their basic necessities like stationary, clothes, shoes, medicine, regular body checkup facilities by coordinating with the people who voluntarily wants to help them. The main aim and mission of our NGO is to make them good human being which has all the qualities which a proud nation wants from its country people. We wants to make them self independent by deeply focusing on their strength and weaknesses and works on them. We not only make them academically strong, but simultaneously arrange different fun and learning sessions like painting competition, fitness events, games events, dance events e.t.c. With time to time we provide them sufficient knowledge of all the fields which they can pursue in the future.
